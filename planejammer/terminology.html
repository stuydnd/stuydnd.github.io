Terminology

  • Plane: a realm of existence
  • Spelljammer: a ship capable of traveling between worlds
  • Lower planes: The Outer Planes associated with Evil
  • Upper planes: The Outer Planes associated with Good
  • Crystal sphere: a gigantic spherical shell housing an entire planetary system
  • Wildspace: the empty vacuum between bodies in a crystal sphere
  • Phlogiston: the flammable, viscous substance between crystal spheres
Let me know if there are any terms I should add!
