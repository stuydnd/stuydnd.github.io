Factions of Sigil

The 15 factions, each led by a Factol, of Sigil often come in to conflict, but together they coexist in order to create a unified city.

  2. Believers of the Source

Factions

Athar

Nickname(s): Defiers, Lost
Philosophy: The "gods" are imposters and frauds. They are not truly divine.
Alignment: Any
Factol: Terrance, male human former priest
HQ: The Shattered Temple, former temple to the forgotten god Aoskar
Plane of Influence: Astral

Athar clerics derive their power from "The Great Unknown".

Believers of the Source

Nickname(s): Godsmen
Philosophy: The multiverse is a forge, and each life is a test. The point of life is to ascend to godhood and then to some mysterious beyond.
Alignment: Any
Factol: Ambar Grove, male half-elf ranger
HQ: The Great Foundry
Plane of Influence: Ethereal

Bleak Cabal

Nickname(s): Madmen, Bleakers, the Cabal
Philosophy: There is no true "meaning" to the multiverse and no "true" beliefs. Any meaning in life has to come from within.
Alignment: Any non-lawful
Factol: Lhar, male half-orc fighter
HQ: The Gatehouse, an insane asylum
Plane of Influence: Pandemonium

Doomguard

Nickname(s): Sinkers
Philosophy: Entropy is inevitable and divine.
Alignment: Any
Factol: Pentar, female human ranger
HQ: Armory
Plane of Influence: Negative Quasielemental Planes

Doomguard members are forbidden from using resurrection or healing spells.

Dustmen

Nickname(s): Dusties, Dustbunnies
Philosophy: This multiverse is not the real one; we're all dead. The goal is to overcome passion and emotion and achieve the nirvana of True Death.
Alignment: Any
Factol: Skall, male wizard
HQ: The Mortuary, where Sigil's dead are buried or cremated
Plane of Influence: Negative Energy Plane

Fated

Nickname(s): Takers, Heartless
Philosophy: The Multiverse belongs to those who hold it. If you can take something, or earn it, you deserve it. Weakness is an excuse.
Alignment: Any except lawful good
Factol: Duke Rowan Darkwood, male human priest/ranger
HQ: Hall of Records, Sigil's tax office
Plane of Influence: Ysgard

Fraternity of Order

Nickname(s): Guvners
Philosophy: The Planes are governed by laws. Laws can be learned. Learn the laws, and you can rule the multiverse
Alignment: Any lawful
Factol: Hashkar, male dwarf sage
HQ: The City Court
Plane of Influence: Mechanus

Free League

Nickname(s): Indeps
Philosophy: No faction knows the whole truth, or should tell anyone what to do. Each person should be free to find his own truth.
Alignment: Any
Factol: None
HQ: The Great Bazaar
Plane of Influence: Outlands

Harmonium

Nickname(s): Hardheads
Philosophy: Everything should live in perfect peace and harmony - under the Harmonium's rule, that is.
Alignment: Any lawful
Factol: Sarin, male human paladin
HQ: City Barracks
Plane of Influence: Arcadia

Mercykillers

Nickname(s): Red Death
Philosophy: Justice is everything. When properly applied, punishment leads to perfection.
Alignment: Any lawful
Factol: Alisohn Nilesia, female tiefling wizard
HQ: City Prison
Plane of Influence: Acheron

Revolutionary League

Nickname(s): Anarchists
Philosophy: The power structures and man-made laws of the multiverse are inherently corrupt. The Factions are keeping everyone from finding the real truth of the multiverse. The only solution is to tear everything down and rebuild with what's left.
Alignment: Any non-lawful
Factol: None
HQ: None
Plane of Influence: Carceri

Sign of One

Nickname(s): Signers
Philosophy: Everyone is at the center of their own multiverse, and reality itself can be altered with the power of imagination.
Alignment: Any, except lawful good or lawful neutral
Factol: Darius, female human diviner
HQ: Hall of Speakers
Plane of Influence: Beastlands

Society of Sensation

Nickname(s): Sensates
Philosophy: Through experience, one gains knowledge. Once everything has been experienced, enlightenment can be reached.
Alignment: Any
Factol: Erin Montgomery, female human priest
HQ: Civic Festhall
Plane of Influence: Arborea

Transcendent Order

Nickname(s): Ciphers
Philosophy: Action without thought is the purest. When you train your body and mind to act as one, you can become one with the Multiverse itself.
Alignment: Any neutral
Factol: Rhys, female tiefling fighter/mage
HQ: Great Gymnasium
Plane of Influence: Elysium

Xaositects

Nickname(s): Chaosmen
Philosophy: There is no order to reality. No truth except Chaos.
Alignment: Any chaotic
Factol: Karan, male Githzerai fighter
HQ: The Hive
Plane of Influence: Limbo
